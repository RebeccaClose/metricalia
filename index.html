METRICALIA is a reading group and workshop space for considering how the overlapping histories of demography, reproductive biology research and the Eugenics movement inform today's so-called 'fertility crisis times' in different national contexts. It forms part of the Imaging/Imagining Reproductive Crisis DOROTHY MSCA project.

The contents of this site was filled in on 10.07.2025 by participants of the CREAR SITUACIONS educational program in an experimental 'net art' animated workshop led by Rebecca Close and Polina Vlasenko.

This site uses local storage not to surveil you or play you but to play with you. This is not quite a cookies ‘accept/reject’ button either, just sharing information about the tools used.
