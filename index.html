METRICALIA is a reading group & workshop space for collectively experimenting with anti-crisis lapses in time and psycadelic trips in the history of counting via satirical Net Art works and counter-readings in the history of reproductive science and demography.

The contents of this site was filled in on 10.07.2025 by participants of the CREAR SITUACIONES#8 educational program at Arts Santa Monica Museum in a workshop led by Rebecca Close and Polina Vlasenko. The activity forms part of the DOROTHY MSCA project Imaging/Imagining Reproductive Crisis.

This site uses local storage not to surveil you or play you but to play with you. This is not quite a cookies ‘accept/reject’ button either, just sharing information about the tools used.
