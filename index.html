METRICALIA is a multi-lingual reading group & experimental workshop space engaging histories of biology, reproductive technologies and demography accross different national contexts.

The contents of this site was filled in on 10.07.2025 by participants of the CREAR SITUACIONES#8 educational program at Arts Santa Monica Museum in a workshop led by Rebecca Close and Polina Vlasenko. The activity forms part of the DOROTHY MSCA project Imaging/Imagining Reproductive Crisis.
